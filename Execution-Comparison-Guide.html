⚖️ Which Execution Framework?

Scrum vs Shape Up vs Dual Track vs OKRs — they solve different problems and combine powerfully.

The Key Distinction

These aren't competing frameworks — they operate at different levels. OKRs set direction (quarterly). Scrum/Shape Up are delivery methods (sprint/cycle). Dual Track adds a discovery layer. Most teams combine 2-3 of these.

🔄
Scrum
HOW we deliver: 2-week sprints with ceremonies
LEVEL: Sprint delivery
🔨
Shape Up
HOW we deliver: 6-week cycles with shaped pitches
LEVEL: Cycle delivery
🛤️
Dual Track
WHAT to deliver: validate before building
LEVEL: Discovery + Delivery
🎯
OKRs
WHY we deliver: quarterly goals and alignment
LEVEL: Quarterly planning

💡 One Sentence Each

OKRs: "This quarter we increase D7 retention to 35%." Dual Track: "We validated that a progress tracker drives retention." Scrum: "Sprint 5: build the progress tracker." Shape Up: "We bet 6 weeks on the progress tracker pitch."

🔗 The Execution Operating System

🎯
Layer 1: OKRs — Direction (Quarterly)
"This quarter: Delight new users. KR: D7 retention from 20% to 35%."
⬇️
🛤️
Layer 2: Dual Track — Validation (Continuous)
"Discovery validated that a progress tracker drives D7 retention. Killed the gamification idea — no evidence."
⬇️
🔄
Layer 3: Scrum or Shape Up — Delivery
"Sprint 5: Build the progress tracker. 21 story points. Demo on Friday." OR "6-week bet: Practice Mode pitch."

🎯 For PM Interviews

Show the full stack: "I set quarterly OKRs aligned with our North Star Metric. I run discovery to validate which ideas actually move those KRs. Only validated ideas enter the delivery backlog, which we execute in 2-week sprints with a clear goal." This answer shows strategic thinking + execution + discipline.
